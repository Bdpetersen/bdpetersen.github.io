About Me

Hello!

Hello! I’m Brandon Petersen, a software engineering student at BYU-Idaho with a passion for solving real-world problems through code, design, and creativity. I’m especially enthusiastic about teaching and aspire to inspire high school students to discover the excitement of building and innovating in the world of software.

Currently, I’m diving into a variety of topics, including C programming, Arduino projects, networking, and databases. Whether it’s creating a joystick-controlled fan system, optimizing SQL queries, or exploring gospel topics through scripture study, I strive to connect my learning with meaningful applications.

Beyond technology, I’m a returned missionary who values purpose-driven work. I enjoy exploring theology, tackling deep questions, and sharing a good laugh to brighten the day.

Feel free to reach out to me at bdpetersen@pacbell.net.
